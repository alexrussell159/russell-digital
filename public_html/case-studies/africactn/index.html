The challenge

AfricaCTN already operated in a high-value niche, but their organic visibility did not reflect the amount of demand in the market. The goal was simple: create a content and SEO system that would increase discoverability for country-specific compliance searches and turn that visibility into qualified leads.

What we focused on

We built out a high-volume content strategy centered on the exact topics people search before filing CTNs: country requirements, document explanations, process questions, and related compliance terms.

Why it mattered

These are not low-intent visitors. Searchers landing on this content are often already close to needing help, which means improved rankings directly impact lead flow and revenue.

What Russell Digital did

2
Targeted high-intent search topics Focused on country-specific CTN requirements, shipping documents, process questions, and supporting educational pages.
3
