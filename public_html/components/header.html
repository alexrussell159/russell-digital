FAQ

Questions we get asked a lot.

How long does SEO take to show results?+
Most clients see meaningful movement in rankings within 60–90 days. Significant traffic and revenue impact typically takes 4–6 months. SEO is a compounding investment — the longer you run it, the better the ROI becomes. We also prioritise quick wins in the first 30 days so you see momentum immediately.
Do you guarantee page 1 rankings?+
No — and you should be wary of any agency that does. Google's algorithm is complex and no one can guarantee positions. What we do guarantee is a transparent process, work we're proud to show you, and a clear ROI methodology. 97% of our clients see page-1 results within 90 days because of how we work, not because we made promises.
What makes Russell Digital different from other SEO agencies?+
Three things: specialists not generalists (your account is handled by people who only do SEO, not account managers who dabble), full transparency (you see everything we do in real time), and a revenue focus (we tie every activity to pipeline and revenue, not just traffic vanity metrics).
Is there a minimum contract length?+
No lock-in contracts. We work on a rolling monthly basis. We do ask for at least 3 months to show meaningful results — SEO is not a one-month activity — but you're never contractually obligated to stay.
What industries do you work with?+
We work across SaaS, eCommerce, professional services, healthcare, fintech and more. Our approach is industry-agnostic at the technical level, but we do assign strategists who have experience in your sector so the content and keyword strategy is grounded in how your buyers actually search.
